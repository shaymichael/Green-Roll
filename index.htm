Green Roll

vegan sushi

At 15 Ahad Ha'am Street is one of the most unique and colorful places in Israel, Green Roll - the first and only vegan sushi restaurant in Israel.
Sushia Green Roll is the first vegan sushi restaurant in Israel, existing since 2015, it started as a small pilot branch on Montefiore Street and has moved to her current residence at Ehad Ha'am street.
Green Roll includes a completely vegan menu - plenty of rolls with everything good from the plant, special sauces that are produced in the restaurant, handmade dim sum, buns, salads, desserts and a variety of other dishes from Asian cuisine.
Delicious, pleasant, full of compassion and with over a decade of experience and knowledge in the field of sushi and cuisine.

Our dishes

The restaurant is completely sterile and welcomes pregnant women who can enjoy fresh, delicious, colorful sushi without fear at all and all this while maintaining a healthy diet. The dishes in the restaurant are varied and stylish - special special dishes, combinations, our main recommended green roll special, party trays for any event or any celebration that contains a quantity of dishes and inside-out rolls that everyone will enjoy. You are welcome to see our delivery menu and order online, pick up in person or of course sit with us at 15 Ahad Ha'am Street in Tel Aviv and experience the magical atmosphere as well. It is tasty, light, comforting and very helpful on the way to a nutritious natural and vegan diet.
